Merachefet
A quiet game about hovering in the void.

What This Is
A small, silent space. No goals. No scores. No winning.

You hover. Particles come to you. If you stay long enough, the void remembers.

That's all.

How to Play
Open the game. Move your cursor (or finger) through the darkness. Hover in one place and wait.

The game rewards nothing except patience.

Why This Exists
Genesis 1:2 says:

"The earth was without form and void, and darkness was over the face of the deep. And the Spirit of God was hovering over the face of the waters."

Before light. Before words. Before fixing or fighting or understanding—there was simply presence.

This game is a small practice of that presence. A place to bring your own void and find that someone is hovering.

Notes
The game is silent by design. Sound may come later, but only as a response to presence, never as a demand.

The void remembers where you've been. Return someday and see.

There is nothing to unlock except what you discover by staying.
